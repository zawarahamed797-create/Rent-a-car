Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer financing on all vehicles?

Yes — most vehicles in our inventory qualify for instalment plans through our 40+ partner banks and leasing companies, subject to approval.

Are your vehicles inspected before sale?

Every vehicle undergoes a 150-point certification covering engine, chassis, electronics and documentation before it is listed for sale.

Can I trade in my current car?

Absolutely. Bring your vehicle to our Faisalabad Road showroom for a same-day, no-obligation valuation.

Do you deliver outside Sheikhupura?

Yes, we offer doorstep delivery across Punjab with a full handover walkthrough included at no extra cost.

What documents do I need to purchase a car?

A valid CNIC and proof of address are required for outright purchase; financing applications require additional income verification documents.
