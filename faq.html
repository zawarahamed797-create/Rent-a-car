Frequently Asked Questions

Buying

Purchasing a Vehicle

Are your vehicles inspected before sale?

Every vehicle undergoes a 150-point certification covering engine, chassis, electronics and documentation before it is listed for sale.

What documents do I need to purchase a car?

A valid CNIC and proof of address are required for outright purchase; financing applications require additional income verification documents.

Can I test drive before buying?

Yes, we encourage every buyer to test drive their shortlisted vehicle at our Faisalabad Road showroom before finalizing a purchase.

Is price negotiable?

Listed prices reflect fair market value, but we're always open to reasonable negotiation after a physical inspection.

Financing

Loans & Instalments

Do you offer financing on all vehicles?

Yes — most vehicles in our inventory qualify for instalment plans through our 40+ partner banks and leasing companies, subject to approval.

How long does approval take?

Most applications receive an initial response within 24 hours, with final approval typically within 3-5 business days.

What is the minimum down payment?

Down payments typically start at 10-20% of the vehicle price, depending on the bank and your credit profile. Use our finance calculator for an estimate.

Selling & Trade-In

Trading Your Vehicle

Can I trade in my current car?

Absolutely. Bring your vehicle to our Faisalabad Road showroom for a same-day, no-obligation valuation.

Do you buy cars outright without a trade-in?

Yes, we purchase vehicles outright at fair market rates, whether or not you're buying a replacement from us.

Delivery & After-Sales

Delivery & Support

Do you deliver outside Sheikhupura?

Yes, we offer doorstep delivery across Punjab with a full handover walkthrough included at no extra cost.

Is there a warranty after purchase?

Extended warranty packages are available on most certified vehicles, covering major mechanical and electrical components.

Who do I contact for servicing after purchase?

Your dedicated relationship manager remains your point of contact for servicing reminders and genuine parts sourcing.
